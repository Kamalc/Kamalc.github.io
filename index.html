◆ About the Game

Journey down the Nile through the Age of the Pharaohs in this relaxing yet challenging mahjong solitaire experience. Clear beautifully illustrated hieroglyph tiles across hundreds of hand-crafted and procedurally generated boards — every one guaranteed solvable.

Designed to be friendly for newcomers and satisfying for puzzle veterans: a gentle guided tutorial, five signature power-ups, and a difficulty curve that grows with you.

◆ Game Features

Eye of Horus — Hint

Reveal a matching pair whenever you get stuck.

Sandstorm Shuffle

Re-arrange the board into a fresh, always-solvable layout.

Ankh of Reversal — Undo

Take back your last move and rethink your path.

Ra’s Blessing — Time Freeze

Pause the timer to plan your next chain of matches.

Pharaoh’s Decree — Auto-Match

Clear a burst of free pairs automatically.

◆ Privacy & Your Data

◆ Delete Your Data

You are always in control of your information. To request deletion of your account and associated data:

  1. Email us with the subject line “Data deletion request”.
  2. Include your in-game display name or Player ID (found in Settings → About).
  3. We will delete your cloud save, account record, and leaderboard entries within 7 days and confirm by email.

You can also remove all local data instantly by uninstalling the game or using Settings → Reset Progress in-app.
